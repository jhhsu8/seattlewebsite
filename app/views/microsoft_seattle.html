Microsoft, Seattle Metropolitan Area

Microsoft (MSFT) is a computer software company founded in 1975. It is headquartered in Redmond, a city located 16 miles east of Seattle. The campus has over 8 million square feet of office space and has about 40,000 employees. The company builds computer software, consumer electronics, computers, and cloud services. Some of its flagship products are Microsoft Windows, Microsoft Office, Xbox, and Microsoft Surface.

In 2017, Microsoft announced plans to replace 12 buildings on the campus with 18 new buildings, adding 2.5 million square feet to hold 8,000 additional employees. Within Seattle, Microsoft has one office space in South Lake Union with more than 1,400 employees.
